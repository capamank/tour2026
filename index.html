The Journey

Seven days of misty mountains, monasteries, and memories.

Jan 10, 2026

Departure from Kolkata

Gather at Asutosh College Main Gate by 5:00 PM. Bus departs for Sealdah Station. Train journey via Darjeeling Mail. Overnight on train with antakshari and card games.

Jan 11, 2026

Arrival in the Queen of Hills

Reach NJP Station. Jeep safari up the winding roads to Darjeeling. Check-in at Hotel Viceroy. Evening walk at the Mall Road and Glenary's Bakery visit.

Jan 12, 2026

Tiger Hill & Zoo

4:00 AM wake up call for sunrise at Tiger Hill. Visit to Himalayan Mountaineering Institute and the Zoo. Afternoon steam engine Joy Ride (Toy Train).

Jan 13, 2026

Transfer to Gangtok

Scenic drive to Sikkim via Teesta River. River rafting stop at Teesta. Arrival in Gangtok. Evening MG Marg exploration and Momo challenge.

Jan 14, 2026

Tsomgo Lake & Baba Mandir

Excursion to the high-altitude Tsomgo Lake (Changu Lake) and Baba Mandir amidst snow. Yak rides available. Grand Gala Dinner and DJ Night back at hotel.

Jan 15, 2026

Return Journey

Morning souvenir shopping. Descent to NJP Station. Board the return train. Farewell speeches and photo exchange on the journey back.
